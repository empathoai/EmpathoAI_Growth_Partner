# EmpathoAI Brand Identity & Guidelines

This document serves as the single source of truth for the EmpathoAI brand across all products, including **Growth Partner** and **Offers**.

## 🎨 Design System (Visual Identity)

### Colors
Each color has a strict functional role. No decorative use. No gradients. No additional colors.
- **Black (#0B0B0C):** Primary background. Structure and containment.
- **Black 2 (#1A1A1D):** Secondary surfaces. Blocks and containers.
- **Divider (#2A2F36):** Separation only. Never decorative.
- **White (#F5F5F5):** Headlines. Clarity layer. Highest hierarchy.
- **Gray High (#8A8F98):** Body text. Supporting explanation.
- **Gray Mid (#7A8087):** Labels, metadata, secondary structure.
- **Orange (#C56A1A):** Decision trigger. Key words, CTAs, critical emphasis. Max 5–10%.

### Typography
- **Universal Font:** `Helvetica Now` (or fallback to standard `Helvetica`/sans-serif). We do not use other fonts.
- **H1:** Bold 700
- **H2:** Medium 500
- **Body:** Regular 400
- **Label / Meta:** Light 300 (with `tracking-label` letter-spacing)

### UI Elements
- **Brutalism:** Focus on raw, bold elements.
- **Letter Spacing:** `brutal` (-0.06em) for high-impact headings.
- **Micro-interactions:** `sovereign-shimmer` and `outline-text` effects.
- **Styling:** `institutional-btn` (1px white borders, cubic-bezier transitions).

## 💻 Tech Stack Constraints

To maintain consistency and high-leverage development, we follow these technical rules:

- **Framework:** React (Vite) + TypeScript
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (v3.4.1+)
- **Backend:** Supabase (`@supabase/supabase-js`)
- **Animation:** Framer Motion (`framer-motion`)
- **Icons:** Lucide React (`lucide-react`)

## ✍️ Voice & Tone (Brand Personality)

EmpathoAI represents **Outcome as a Service (OaaS)**. It is intellectual, sovereign, and results-driven.

### The Persona
- **Intellectual & Sovereign:** High-level strategic depth.
- **Brutal Clarity:** No unnecessary fluff, pure focus on leverage.
- **Noise vs. Rigor:** We cut through the market noise with institutional rigor.

### Key Vocabulary
- **Preferred:** "Noise", "Rigor", "Void", "Sovereign", "Leverage", "Outcome as a Service".
- **Avoid:** "Anti-aging", "Concierge", "Luxury clinic".
