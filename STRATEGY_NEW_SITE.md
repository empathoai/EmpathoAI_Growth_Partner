## 2. Strategy for the "New Sovereign Site"

Instead of modifying the current codebase, we will build the **EmpathoAI Sovereign Portal v3**. This allows for a clean break from "Brutalism" into "Institutional Editorial Minimalism".

### A. Technical Stack 2026
*   **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) for superior SEO and Server Components.
*   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + Radix UI (Primitives) for sophisticated, accessible UI components.
*   **Typography:** [Libre Baskerville] or [Playfair Display] (for Editorial Serif) + [Inter] or [Inter Tight] (for Executive Sans).
*   **Animation:** Shared Layout Transitions (Framer Motion) that feel "Heavy/Solid" rather than "Bouncy/Fast".

### B. Structural Blueprint (The 3 Layers)
The site will be structured literally around the **EmpathoAI Growth Architecture™** layers from the Vault:
1.  **Layer 01 (Foundations):** Focus on Offer Engineering and Positioning as the primary entry point.
2.  **Layer 02 (System Design):** Visualization of the "Integrated System" (Acquisition + Monetization).
3.  **Layer 03 (Growth Engineering):** The "Control Center" approach, showing modeling and margin optimization.

### C. Visual Direction: "The Boardroom Aesthetic"
*   **Negative Space:** Radical use of white/light-gray space to symbolize "Clarity".
*   **Graphic Language:** Clean, technical diagrams (Grid-based architecture), avoiding generic gradients.
*   **Photography:** Architectural, high-end, structural (symbolizing the "Architect" persona).
